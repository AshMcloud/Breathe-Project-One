Media To Relax To

It's importance of taking a moment to relax in life. Some times it's by putting on your favourite tunes, reading a book, or by watching a film or video

If you're struggling to relax in the evening, maybe it's best to turn the tv or computer off a little earlier in the day, listen to music or read a book. Below I've included a quote on bluelight to show its effects on the body.

"More so than any other color, blue light messes with your body’s ability to prepare for sleep because it blocks a hormone called melatonin that makes you sleepy. Bottom line: You’re less drowsy than usual at night, and it takes you longer to fall asleep."

WebMD article on bluelight F.lux - a program that helps to mitigate the negative affects of bluelight If you're not tired of fish puns yet...
